================================================================================
SemEval-2014 Task-3 Training Data Licenses
================================================================================

SemEval-2014 Task-3 includes data from publicly available corpora whose licenses
allow redistribution.  The following portions of the training data are covered
under their respective licenses:

Newswire data in wikinews.txt is provided by WikiNews and released under the Creative Commons
Attribution 2.5 License: http://creativecommons.org/licenses/by/2.5/

Newswire data in msr-ssla.txt is provided as a part of the Microsoft
Research Paraphrase Corpus and released under the MSR-SSLA
http://research.microsoft.com/en-us/downloads/607d14d9-20cd-47e3-85bc-a2f65cd28042/License.txt

Newswire data in ppdb.txt is provided from PPDB and released under the Creative
Commons Attribution 3.0 Unported License:
http://creativecommons.org/licenses/by/3.0/

Newswire data in reuters.txt is provided from the Reuters-21578, Distribution
1.0 corpus and released under their license:
http://kdd.ics.uci.edu/databases/reuters21578/README.txt

Travel data is provided as a part of the Open ANC and released under the Open
ANC license: http://www.americannationalcorpus.org/OANC/license.txt (also
included)

Lexicographic and Descriptive data is provided as a part of the English
Wikipedia and released under the Creative Commons Attribution-ShareAlike 3.0
License: http://creativecommons.org/licenses/by-sa/3.0/

Scientific data included in the test data is provided as a part of the English
Wikipedia and released under the Creative Commons Attribution-ShareAlike 3.0
License:
http://creativecommons.org/licenses/by-sa/3.0/

Review data is provided by the Stanford Large Network Dataset Collection.

Community Question Answering (CQA) data is provided as a part of the Yahoo!
Answers Dataset.
